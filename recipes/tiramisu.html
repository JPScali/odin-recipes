Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert made of ladyfinger pastries (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of cream, sugar, and mascarpone, and topped with cocoa powder. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Ingredients

  • One pack of savoiardi
  • 400ml mascarpone
  • 300ml heavy cream
  • Powder sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • 600ml cool esspresso coffee
  • 1oz Ammaretto liquer

Steps

  1. Whip cream to stiff peaks.
  2. Fold in mascarpone and vanilla extract.
  3. Sweeten mixture with powdered sugar to taste. Set aside.
  4. Combine coffee and Ammaretto in a bowl.
  5. Dip savoiardi in coffee mixture one at a time and layer flat in serving dish. Conitnue until there is one flat, complete layer in dish.
  6. Add a smooth layer of mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of savoiardi.
  7. Add another layer of savoiardi and mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top.
  8. Dust top with cocoa powder.
