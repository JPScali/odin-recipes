Fresh Pasta

Description

Fresh pasta is a delicate, un-dried noodle made from a dough of flour, eggs, and sometimes water or olive oil, then kneaded, rested, and shaped into various forms

Ingredients

  • 1 cup type-00 flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons water (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Make a well in the centre of flour and add beaten egg yolks.
  3. Slowly combine flour and eggs until a dough forms.
  4. Knead dough on floured surface for 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Wrap dough in saran wrap and leave to rest for one hour.
  6. Roll out dough by hand or with pasta rolling machine to desired thickness.
  7. Cut into strips of desired thickness and lenght.
